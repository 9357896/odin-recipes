Cuzcuz is a very simple recipe which involves no more than three main ingredients, making it a perfect choice for when you're too busy or lazy to cook something complicated.

Without further ado, let's dive into this quick and easy recipe:

ingredients

  • 2 tea-cups flocked corn flour
  • 1 tea-cup water (approximately)
  • 1 tea-spoon salt

Steps

  1. in a bowl, mix the flocked corn flour and the salt
  2. slowly add in the water while whisking with your hands
  3. cover the bowl for 20 minutes and let it rest
  4. after the 20 minutes, whisk the mixture again for better texture
  5. It should be ready after about 10 minutes over water vapor
