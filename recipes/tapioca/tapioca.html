Tapioca

Look at this delicious delicacy!! Let's prepare it!!

Tapioca is a light, healthy and extremely versatile dish that allows you to use just about anything you want as filling.

Here are the ingredients and how to prepare it:

ingredients

  • 500g of tapioca flour
  • As much salt as you want
  • Just about any filling you like

Steps

  1. mix the salt with the flour
  2. with a spoon, slowly add the flour on a frying pan
  3. cover the whole pan with the flour until you make a thin layer
  4. add the fillings on top of the flour
  5. after the layer is somewhat hard and crusty, fold it over the filling and wait 1 minute.
  6. take it directly to a plate and you're ready to go!!
